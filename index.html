About Us

Agherton Village is situated on the outskirts of Portstewart a close drive to Royal Portrush Golf Club and the 2019 Golf Open.

At Agherton Village we have forty five 2, 3, 4 and 6 bedroom apartments, each have single bedrooms with washbasin and a shared bathroom/s with shower & toilet.

With the exception of the 6 bedroom houses, the apartments have open-plan kitchen/dining areas & lounges.
